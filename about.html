Rafaela Nunes, also known as Nenhures, was born in Lisbon in 1991. She is an artist and researcher currently studying for a PhD in Multimedia Art at the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Lisbon. She is a fellow at the Interactive Technologies Institution - LARSyS and eGames Lab in the fields of arts and interactive media, where she develops a practice-based research. She has worked extensively in the artistic field and has a record of various exhibitions, artistic residencies, published artist‚Äôs books, as well as having worked as an educator in visual arts.


Through her work, she poses a critical view on our relationship with wilderness, presenting artificialised natures using various media like painting, sculpture, installation and digital media. By rethinking and blurring the dichotomies of the natural and the artificial, the real and the fictional, the authentic and the fake, she explores the ambiguity and the plasticity of the boundaries between what we perceive as reality and what we construct as representations of it.
